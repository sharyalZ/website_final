Saba Yaqoob

Artist, Graphic Designer

​Saba Yaqoob is a multidisciplinary artist from Pakistan based in Paris, France. Her painting style is naïve, touches emotions; done in a variety of subjects and mediums like acrylics and watercolour. In design, she inclines towards packaging and branding. Her style is minimal and contemporary yet retains a unique touch of her roots.
