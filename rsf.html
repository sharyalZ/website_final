RSF Rebranding

Rebranding of RSF's current brand identity. Taking into account the underlying message behind the NGO and paying homage to all those journalists who have disappeared, been jailed or lost their lives. In addition, creation of a special edition packaging kit for RSF's history, including the basic tools of a journalist. Packaging has been done in isometry due to the constraints of the 2020 pandemic.
